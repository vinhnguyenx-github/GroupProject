About China

China offers a mix of ancient history and modern city life. Visitors see old temples, historic walls, and fast growing cities.

The country covers mountains, deserts, rivers, and coastlines. Major cities such as Beijing, Shanghai, and Xi'an are popular for travel.

Food & Cuisine

Chinese food has many regional styles. Well known dishes include dumplings, Peking duck, hotpot, fried rice, and noodles.

Flavors range from mild to very spicy. Travelers visit local restaurants and street stalls to try different dishes.

Things to Do

Historical Sites: Walk on the Great Wall, visit the Forbidden City, and see the Terracotta Army.

Cities: Explore Shanghai's skyline, Beijing's old streets, and modern business areas.

Nature: Visit Zhangjiajie, Guilin's karst mountains, and the Yellow Mountains.

Experiences: Ride high speed trains, join local markets, and enjoy traditional festivals.
