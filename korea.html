About Korea

South Korea is a vibrant country where ancient traditions blend beautifully with cutting-edge modern life. With dynamic cities, scenic mountains, and a rich cultural heritage, Korea offers a wide range of travel experiences.

Visitors can explore peaceful temples, bustling markets, and high-tech districts like Seoul’s Gangnam. Korea’s mix of history, technology, and creativity continues to attract travelers worldwide.

Food & Cuisine

Korean cuisine is known for bold flavors, fermented dishes, and shared meals. Popular foods include kimchi, bibimbap, bulgogi, tteokbokki, Korean fried chicken, and hot stone stews.

Street food plays a huge role in Korean culture. Night markets offer treats like hotteok, fish cakes, and tornado potatoes—perfect for exploring local flavors.

Things to Do

Nature & Landscapes: Hike Seoraksan, explore Jeju Island’s beaches and volcanic formations, and stroll through cherry blossom parks in spring.

Cities: Visit Seoul’s palaces, wander through Hongdae and Myeongdong, and experience Busan’s coastal culture.

Culture & History: Tour Gyeongbokgung Palace, visit the DMZ, and explore traditional hanok villages.

Modern Attractions: Dive into K-Pop, tech districts, arcades, themed cafés, and shopping centers like COEX Mall.
