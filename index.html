Popular Destinations

Vietnam

Explore vibrant cities, diverse landscapes, and rich cultural traditions across Vietnam.

Best Time to Visit: November to April

Must-Try Food: Pho, Bun Cha, Bun Rieu, Chao Suon

Top Activities: Ha Long Bay cruise, exploring Hoi An Ancient Town, street food tours in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City

Transportation: Domestic flights, trains, buses, motorbikes

Korea

Experience modern cities, scenic mountains, and rich cultural heritage across Korea.

Best Time to Visit: March to May, September to November

Must-Try Food: Kimchi, Bibimbap, Korean BBQ, Tteokbokki

Top Activities: Visit Seoul palaces, explore Jeju Island, shop in Myeongdong, hike in national parks

Transportation: KTX trains, subways, buses, domestic flights

Japan

Enjoy historic temples, advanced technology, and stunning natural scenery across Japan.

Best Time to Visit: March to May, October to November

Must-Try Food: Sushi, Ramen, Tempura, Takoyaki

Top Activities: Visit Kyoto shrines, explore Tokyo, see Mount Fuji, enjoy cherry blossoms

Transportation: Shinkansen trains, subways, buses

Singapore

Explore a modern city with iconic landmarks, clean streets, and a mix of Asian cultures.

Best Time to Visit: February to April

Must-Try Food: Hainanese chicken rice, Laksa, Chili crab, Satay

Top Activities: Visit Marina Bay Sands, Gardens by the Bay, Sentosa, Chinatown

Transportation: MRT trains, buses, taxis

China

Discover ancient history, modern megacities, and diverse landscapes across China.

Best Time to Visit: April to October

Must-Try Food: Dumplings, Peking duck, Hotpot, Mapo tofu

Top Activities: Visit the Great Wall, explore Beijing and Shanghai, see Zhangjiajie mountains

Transportation: High-speed trains, domestic flights, buses
